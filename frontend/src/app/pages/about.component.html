About PollPulse

A focused student voice platform for university feedback and class leadership.

PollPulse helps Air University CS students take part in CR/GR voting and share useful feedback about teachers, courses, events and campus facilities. It keeps the experience simple, secure and class-specific.

AU email access Section-based voting Sentiment reporting
How it works

Use PollPulse in four easy steps

The website is made for students, teachers and administration to understand feedback clearly.

01
Register/Login

Students register with their official Air University email, such as 2502077@students.au.edu.pk.

02
Select class details

The student profile is connected with degree, year and section so voting stays class-specific.

03
Vote or give feedback

Students can vote for CR/GR or open dedicated feedback pages for teacher, course, library, transport and more.

04
View final results

After polls close, final winners, vote percentages and feedback sentiment results become available.

What can students do?

Students can participate in elections, submit feedback, and help the department identify what is working well and what needs improvement.

CR/GR VotingVote only in your own class section.
Teacher FeedbackRate teaching quality, punctuality and communication.
Course FeedbackShare views about content, difficulty and assessments.
Sentiment ResultFeedback comments are summarized as positive, neutral or negative.
